About Us

Beycan Abacus Training, in partnership with Indian Abacus, offers a unique abacus program designed to enhance mathematical skills and boost brainpower in children aged 4-13 years. Our holistic approach focuses on improving memory, concentration, creativity, and problem-solving abilities.

Our Classes

Our structured curriculum is designed to build mathematical skills progressively, starting with basic techniques and advancing to complex calculations. We combine the use of abacus and visual arithmetic to help children master mental math.

Benefits of Abacus Training

  • Improved Academic Performance
  • Boosted Confidence
  • Holistic Brain Development
  • Enhanced Memory and Concentration
  • Strong Problem-Solving Skills
